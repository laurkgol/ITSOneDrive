How OneDrive Works

OneDrive replaces Box as the World Bank Group’s solution for file storing and sharing. It also integrates seamlessly with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and other productivity applications so your content is where you need it, when you need it. You can share files securely with anyone—inside or outside the organization—for collaboration on the go.
